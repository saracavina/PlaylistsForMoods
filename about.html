Hi! I'm Sara, I'm from Italy and I'm a Communication Sciences student at Alma Mater Studiorum - University of Bologna. I made this site as a final project for the course "Informatica Umanistica" (Informatics for Humanities) during the academic year 2021/2022 to share my love for music.

I've always believed that the right music together with the right atmosphere has such a big impact on our brain that it can make anyone feel wherever, whenever and however they want. And that's how this project was born.
It's a little something you can try whenever you want or feel the need to be somewhere else than where you are, even if just for a little bit.
Hope you enjoy and thank you for visiting this site, feel free to stay as long as you want!

Send me an e-mail if you have any questions regarding the site, playlists or social media accounts.
