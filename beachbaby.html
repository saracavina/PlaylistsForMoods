BEACH BABY

playlist name: beach baby
listening time: 52 minutes.
number of songs: 13.
place: the seaside.
weather: cloudy.
time of the day: morning or early afternoon.
further sounds: waves crashing, wind blowing, seagulls squawking.
scents: salt, lime, coconut, eucalyptus, tangerine.

scroll down for more

possible scenario:

you're having a walk on the beach, the weather is cloudy. you're listening to the waves crashing on the shore and you can hear the chorus of seagulls as they glide in the wind. you can hear the soft breeze of the wind on your face and you can feel the fresh, clear water and the wet sand with your bare feet. it's just you and nature. you're the main character in this life. everything is fine.

enhance your listening experience:

if you're lucky enough to be near a beach; go there and have a walk while listening to this playlist. if not, just lay back on your bed or couch, close your eyes, light up some candles and imagine being on that beach.

if you don't know much about candles, or scents in general, "Pink Sands" and "Beach Escape" by Yankee Candle will work just fine.

Otherwise, you can mix some lime, eucalyptus and tangerine essential oils in a diffuser and enjoy that.
