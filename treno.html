ON A TRAIN AND IT'S RAINING

you're sitting on a train when it suddenly starts raining. and just like that you suddenly see the same old familiar countryside through the lens of dozens of small raindrops on the train window. you can hear the rain pouring down while the train keeps speeding up. after several minutes, the rain slowly stops and you can see a rainbow in the sky. you've almost arrived at your destination.

This is a playlist specifically made for when you're on a train or bus and it's actually raining outside. But if you're home and you just miss travelling, you can light up a candle that specifically "smells like rain" (and I'll tell you: there actually are a few candles that really do smell like rain out there), like "Rain" by Village Candle or "November Rain" by Yankee Candle.
