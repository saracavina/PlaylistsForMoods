IN THE MOUNTAIN WOODS

you're having a walk in the mountain woods with your friends. you all decided to take a break from the chaos of the city and spend a day in nature. you can hear the wind blowing through the trees and the fresh smell of pine trees. you keep walking and you find a stream. you put a hand in that stream and you feel the fresh water running through your fingers. you then cross the stream and find an open space, so you all decide to sit down and rest for a bit, before going back home.

I made this playlist while being in the mountain woods with my friends, so these are all songs that remind me of that. and I really suggest you to take a break from everything from time to time and go have a walk in the woods or in nature, in general.

but if you aren't in the mood or if you aren't able to go, there are so many candles that smell like you're in the woods. Some of them are: "Royal Oak" by Price's Candles and "Evergreen Mist" by Yankee Candle, but the choice is very wide and you'll find the right scent for you.
